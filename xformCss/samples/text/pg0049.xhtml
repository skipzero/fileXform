Possessive Nouns
1.
To make a singular noun possessive,
add an apostrophe (’) +
-s
to the end
of the noun.
The
baby’s
room is small.
Lisa’s
car is green.
2.
To make most plural nouns possessive,
add an apostrophe (’) after the
-s

babies’
room is small.
My
